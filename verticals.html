Verticals

Editorial

The editorial cell comes up with the content that is to be presented to the audience. We come up with ideas, research on them, explicate and debate on them, form notions, maybe twist the dogma, do more research, more deliberations, more judgements, write on them, edit them, re-write, re-edit them, maybe scratch them or present them, and reiterate the routine till we are content with the output.

Design

The design cell dolls up the raw text and comes up with formats and elements that make the notion of the text enticing to read and easier to interpret. We also integrate individual write-ups to make it into one preeminent product or series following an appealing theme.

Finance

The finance cell comes up with the funds for publication, promulgation, and circulation of the magazines to earnest readers by bringing in sponsors. We hitch the outside market to the magazine and the campus and fixate on increasing our reach in R-land and beyond.

Web Development

Web Development cell works to increase the online presence of the magazine by increasing the ease of accessing the content we produce, not only to the campus inhabitants but to anyone around the world. We love to explore inventive methods to present the textual information, so as to make it as gripping as we could.
